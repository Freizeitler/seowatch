Welcome! This is my personal test case to find out if search engines can fetch contents of different popular frontend techniques. I'll update and maintain this document as regularly as possible.

This page is online since: 18.05.2015
First result was tracked: 20.05.2015
Last update: 31.08.2015

To check the results grab the phrase below the content-section of a box and copy it into the search bar of the search engine of your choice. Use site:-search preferably. Or simply press the result label for a quick Google check.
Btw., the results indicated with works, not working and pending refer all to Google.
